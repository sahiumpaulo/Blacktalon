Relics

Relics are items that can either give you attributes (skills, dodge, hp, mp, etc.), a modifier (double attack, ice strike attack, etc.) or a GENERAL BENEFIT (boss loot, elite chance, etc.).
They can be obtained through different methods, detailed below.
Also, their benefits can be upgraded from tier 1 up to tier 3 by using the Chaos Compass.
